# E017 - Life Priorities and Compass (P5)

## Goal

Connect player-stated priorities and Life Compass dimensions to decision previews, contextual quizzes, and stakeholder reactions. Choices should show life impact, not only dollar deltas.

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Life priorities persist across chapter and affect decision previews
- [ ] Life Compass dimensions show goal deltas on each choice

- [ ] Stakeholder reactions reference specific player actions, not generic period summaries
